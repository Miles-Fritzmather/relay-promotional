The privacy promise

We never see anything.
By design, not by policy.

Every promise below is built into how Relay works — not buried in a contract you have to take on trust. Disconnect from the internet and it keeps running, exactly the same.

§ 01

Inference is local.

Every AI inference runs on the processor in your own office. No prompt, no document, and no client detail ever leaves the machine. Disconnect from the internet and watch it keep working — nothing to take on faith.

§ 02

Storage is local.

Briefs, contracts, depositions, and the matter knowledge base live in an encrypted vault on your disk. We don't have credentials, and we couldn't decrypt them if subpoenaed.

§ 03

Training is prohibited.

Your work product is never used to train, fine-tune, or evaluate any model — ours or anyone else's. Telemetry is opt-in, anonymous, and limited to crash reports.

§ 04

Compliance is the default state.

Relay's architecture maps cleanly to ABA Model Rule 1.6 and the analogous state rules. No additional configuration, no enterprise plan, no "private mode" toggle to forget.
